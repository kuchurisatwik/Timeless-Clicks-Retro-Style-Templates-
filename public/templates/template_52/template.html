SPOTTED IN
GOTHAM CITY!
By Clark Kent
Daily Planet
Gotham City- Yesterday afternoon, Gothamites looked up to the sky, not to catch a glimpse of the Dark Knight, but to stare in awe at the Kryptonian from Metropolis. So many Kryptonians have been known to cause so much death and destruction, that many citizens of Gotham were concerned and sought shelter in nearby storefronts. A majority of the city were ecstatic to see Superman, one witness stated "He's like a glimmer of light in our dark city.". Some curious onlookers even took many snapshots of the hero, others took selfies. While a crowd gathered below, Superman hovered above twirling slowly over the center of Gotham. One can assume he was using his famed X-ray vision to search for something, or someone. Being in Gotham on business myself, I followed Superman to Gotham Harbor. Our alien savior entered what appeared to be an empty warehouse. I peeked through a window to see what I occurred inside. Alien Space ships were lined in rows and Military vehicles were parked in the front of the warehouse. Army personnel were standing around a table arguing with Superman.
